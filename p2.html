MOBILE COMPUTER TYPES:
Type Feature Benefit
Handheld • Combines functionality of a PC and a scanner into a single device • Extends organizational knowledge into the field
Wearable • Sophisticated voice-directed applications • Improves performance and accuracy by streamlining the picking process
Vehicle-Mounted • Full PC functionality • Makes it easier for employees to work under extreme conditions
Tablets • High-speed Wi-Fi connections that supports the latest WLAN or WWAN networks • Promises superior performance with the ability to work from virtually any location
Smartphones • Always-on availability of enterprise applications • Gives instant access to the latest information, enables immediate updates, and relays critical data
Hazardous Environment • Combines global certifications with cutting-edge technologies • Ensures compliance with industry and governmental regulations in the most volatile environments
